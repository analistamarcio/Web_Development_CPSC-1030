Drones are small flying vehicles in which they are controlled remotely by an operator. The simplest models of these glider robots are controlled using a special application installed on a smartphone or tablet.

The more complex models of drones, on the other hand, tend to have their own control system working on an exclusive radio frequency - an idea very similar to that of remote control models and airplane models.

Drone pilots here in Canada must follow the rules of the Canadian Aviation Regulation (CARs).

I am still a beginner in this area and I have a long way to go in learning and controlling these "toys", but I have already become a lover of the practice of flying drones. You can contact me by email.
